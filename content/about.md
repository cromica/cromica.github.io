**This is my personal blog.** Everything written here is written by me, and is my opinion. This is my content, and I am the person responsible. Not the company I work for right now, not my colleagues, not anyone else.

**Principal Engineering Manager at Microsoft**

As a Principal Engineering Manager at Microsoft, I lead large scale platform initiatives in the identity and security space part of Microsoft Entra product suite. Most recently, I led the multi-year deprecation of a legacy identity credential management platform, coordinating multiple teams to migrate milions monthly active users to a modern, secure backend. 
I am now building and leading the federation platform team, enabling tens of millions of users to authenticate seamlessly across organizational boundaries.

**Approach to Engineering Leadership**

My leadership philosophy centers on clarity of mission, sustainable pace, and ownership at every level. I focus on:

* **Technical strategy** at scale, coordinating complex migrations, modernizing platform architecture, and making infrastructure decisions that carry forward for years.
* **Team effectiveness** building trust across large, distributed engineering groups and fostering psychological safety so that people can do their best work.
* **Sustainable engineering practices** building cultures of testing, observability, and learning that outlast any single project.
* **Mentoring and growth** developing engineering talent at all levels and creating pathways for strong people to take on bigger challenges.

**Background & Open Source**

I've worked across engineering leadership, technical architecture, and organizational development in both startup and enterprise environments. I've contributed to the open-source ecosystem through libraries and frameworks designed to improve developer experience, and I share knowledge through writing, speaking, and community engagement.
