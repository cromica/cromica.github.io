# Squad Ceremonies

## Available ceremonies

### Editorial Readiness Review
- **when:** before publishing important written content
- **facilitator:** Mustang
- **participants:** Theodora, Roque, Dancer
- **goal:** ensure editorial quality, promotion readiness, and career-positioning fit

### Platform Evolution Review
- **when:** before major Hugo or publishing workflow changes
- **facilitator:** Mustang
- **participants:** Sevro, Theodora
- **goal:** balance platform improvements with editorial workflow stability

### Monthly Brand Signal Check
- **when:** manual
- **facilitator:** Roque
- **participants:** Dancer, Mustang
- **goal:** review whether recent content supports audience growth and career goals
