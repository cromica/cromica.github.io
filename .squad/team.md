# Squad Team

## Project Context

- **User:** Romulus Crisan

- **Secondary mission:** Evolve and maintain the Hugo platform that powers the site.
- **Growth mission:** Promote Romulus's personal brand and shape content for maximum career impact and executive outreach.

## Routing Notes

- Content drafts start with **Theodora** for editorial review.
- Strategic essays or high-stakes posts get **Mustang** final sign-off.
- Hugo/site changes route to **Sevro**.
- Promotion plans and distribution route to **Roque**.
- Career-impact framing, executive audience fit, and recruiter relevance route to **Dancer**.
- Cross-functional work starts with **Mustang** and fans out from there.
